Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Privacy is a fundamental right, and we take it seriously. This policy explains how Charades handles your information.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The Charades mobile application is designed to function entirely on your device. We do not have a backend server, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information from the app.

  • No account registration is required.
  • No personal identifiers (emails, names, phone numbers) are collected.
  • No location tracking is used.

2. Data Storage

All app data, such as your customized game settings or team names, is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone and is deleted if you uninstall the application.

3. Third-Party Services

While the app itself does not collect data, we use certain third-party services for hosting and distribution:

  • GitHub Pages: This support website and privacy policy are hosted on GitHub Pages. Usage of this site is subject to the GitHub Privacy Statement, which may collect server logs and basic web traffic data.
  • App Stores: Downloading the app and managing subscriptions is handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, which have their own privacy policies.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
